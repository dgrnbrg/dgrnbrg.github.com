About the Artist

The DHC (Deltoidal Hexecontahedron) is a sixty-sided sphere covered in 180 superbright RGB LEDs. It can display arbitrary colors and patterns on its surface. The DHC is my followup to the Eye of Gorlack. After the Eye, I wanted to make an entirely spherical LED light and replicate it many times, so that a room could be illuminated by a swarm of them. I am currently working on several features for the project I will announce in the future.
